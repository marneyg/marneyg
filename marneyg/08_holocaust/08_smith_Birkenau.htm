         Birkenau was a harsh camp.   It was built by the Nazi's leader Hitler.  The Nazi would kill  Jews, Russian POWs, even children.

      

           Birkenau was an extermination-concentration camp.  They killed the children when they got there.  Birkenau at the time was 40 Km.  It was also a labor, transit camp rolled into one.  They filled with Russian POWs, it was known as the SS "utopia", in upper Silesia.  Birkenau was the world's largest grave yard.  The victims were scattered through out the fields, in small ponds, a lake, or in the river.  Krakow was 37 miles away it was the capital of the German occupants in Poland.       

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                The platforms lead to the barracks.  The Barracks are houses generally fitting 3 people, but because of the size of Birkenau they had to fit 4 people.  Men and women are separated into different barracks.  The children were just killed when they got there.  9 out of 10 people were Jewish the other were Russian POWs, Poles,  etc. from May 14, 1944-June 8, 1944 there were 437,400 people living in Birkenau.  Auschwitz was found in 1270.  They were known as Auschwitz, Birkenau, and Monowitz.  Oswiecim (Auschwitz) is now a factory town with 50,000 residents.  Oswiecim was captured on September 6, 1947.  

          April 1940 Rudolph Hoss was appointed head commander of Auschwitz.  Hitler commanded the construction of Birkenau, shortly after Hoss was appointed head commander of Auschwitz.  Birkenau was the Nazi's largest concentration camp.  It was finished on April 21,1940.  In May 1940 the Poles were thrown out of  Birkenau.  300 Jews were drug into building the concentration camp of  Birkenau.
 

      
       There were 21-2.5 million Jews were killed.  Auschwitz was made into 3 different camps.  Auschwitz 1, 2, and 3.  Their real names are Auschwitz, Birkenau, and Monowitz.  After September 1941 the poison gas became more popular in the concentration camp.  Before that they had 10,000 people in the camp.  Then the mass killings, suicide, and torture.  Himmler ordered bigger more complex camps.  They were suppose to hold 100,000 people.
