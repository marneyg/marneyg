          Harry J. Heder Jr. is a survivor who has been for ever changed and traumatized after his experiences from being an inmate at Buchenwald. Here is his story: "over fifty years ago I went through a set of experiences that I have never been able to shake from my mind. I was on top of the vehicle as we entered the camp, we drove through 4 rows of barbed wire. I got a clue, a clue as to what we had come upon, but we had no real comprehension a to what would assault our senses for the next few hours or days or even weeks."  Heder has been stuck in shock for over 50 years. Once a year around Spring time he has terrible flash backs from being tortured and having seen so many people die.
