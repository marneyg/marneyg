Total Killed
On November 9th, 1938 (Kristallnacht) many people died. Twelve million to be exact. Six million Jewish adults, one million Jewish children, and five million others. Thirty-thousand Jewish men were sent to concentration camps. More than one-thousand synagogues were either burned down or totally destroyed (unable to be used). In just one town ninety-six Jews were killed (at least that's what the Nazis said) and many others were wounded.
