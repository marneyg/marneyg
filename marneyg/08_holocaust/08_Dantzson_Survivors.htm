Simha Rotem (who is now 84) was one of the few people to survive the Warsaw Ghetto Uprising. He was also the only one ,that survived, who fought the Nazis in the 1943 Uprising. He also survived World War 2 in Poland's forests. Rotem has also stared in the movie "The Last Fighters".

         "The Last Fighters" is a movie about the huge struggles and emotions of the survivors of the Warsaw Ghetto Uprising. Simha Rotem is one of the six survivors staring in this film. Mostly all of the survivors are in their eighties and now live in Israel.
