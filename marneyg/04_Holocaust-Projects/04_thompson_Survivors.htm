Before they went to camps and when Hitler came in power.  Jews could not do things like be in the streets, teach in  public schools, have jobs that have anything to do with the government.  Jewish children had to sit in the last rows of desk in the class room.  Soon Jews couldn't even be a citizen because of a law that was passed called the Nuremberg Racial Laws  causing Jews to loose their citizenship  immedtely.  So 100,000 Jews left Germany.  But even though they didn't want the Jews in their country the Germans made it hard for them to leave. also few countries would let the Jews in their country

If the Nazis did not need any of the Jews they shot them the Jews.  Some of the Nazis called the Jews dogs.  If a Jew gets hurt and the Nazis know it they hang the Jew that is hurt.  The Nazis also hang you for any little thing.  What ever number went to work the same number has to come back from work. When Jews get back from there work they get a bowl of soup and the soup is made of water, grass, and sand. Jew work for12 hours. At night they sleep on wood.
