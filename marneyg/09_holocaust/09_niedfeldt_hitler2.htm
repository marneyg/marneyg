Adolf Hitler

 
 

Around 6:30 pm on April 20th, 1889 Adolf Hitler was born in a village in Austria just across the German Bavaria. Hitler was enrolled in school at age six. When Hitler started school his father retired, his father was 58 years old, a little after that his mother had baby, in that time it was 1896.

 

In 1900 Hitler became better at being an artist. He did well and was eligible to go in an art school. Later Hitler accepted his fathers decision witch was to go to a school called Realschule but failed.

On Saturday morning wile Hitler’s father was on a walk, on January 3rd, 1903,his father died. Hitler’s father was 65 years old and Hitler was 13.
  

Two years after Hitler’s father died, Hitler was 15 and had to repeat the years of school. At age 18 Hitler received his father’s inheritance will and he moved to Vienna. After he got to Vienna, Hitler sent an application to the Vienna academy but they rejected his offer.

In 1905 Adolf Hitler was 16 years old and he decided to drop out of school. Hitler’s mother wanted him to learn a trade or get a job, but Hitler was disgusted of the thought of working.

In 1906 Hitler was able to visit Vienna but couldn’t go to school there. Hitler’s mother had breast cancer and Dr. Edward Bloch was her doctor. Dr. Bloch was a Jewish doctor who served the poor. The doctor used a painful drug during the operation and as a result she died on December 21, 1907.
 

 Hitler became homeless and survives on his father’s small legacy in an orphan’s home. When he became homeless he was 18 years old, in Vienna.
 

Hitler was used as a German spy and was ordered to attend a meeting of the German Worker’s Party (GWP) in 1919. Anton Dexler was the leader of the party. Hitler was not impressed when he found out of their idea of Anti-Semitism. After Hitler joined at age 30, he tried to make them successful and had the party put an advertisement in the Anti-Semitism newspaper.
 

On November 9, 1923, Hitler and the Nazi party had a Beer Hall Putsch. After that he was in a trail and was sentenced for 5 years in prison, he only spent 9 months in prison. While he was in prison he wrote a book called Mein Kamf, it was mostly an autobiography but he didn’t write about his hatred of Jews.

  
 

World war 1 was started by the assassination by a Serb. Arch Duke Franz Ferdinand was the person that was assassinated. Adolf Hitler left Vienna and went to Munich in May 1913. Hitler left to avoid military service and at the time his aunt was dieing. After he moved the police were at his house with a notice that he was going to be in the army.

  
 

Adolf Hitler was in training for two months. After training he was posted in Ypres and saw saw first combat against the Belgians and the British. Many times Hitler almost died. During the war he was placed in trenches. Hitler and everyone else were eager to fight because they thought it would be a short was. Millions died through the war and it was longer then they thought.

  
 

An enemy shell injured Hitler in October 1916. After he recovered he was blinded in a mustard gas attack in October 1918.
