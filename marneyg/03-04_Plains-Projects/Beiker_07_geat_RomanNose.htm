 Roman Nose

On July 24, 1865, at Platter Bridge at the state of Nebraska, came Roman Nose to his first battle against the white men. He led a huge number of Cheyenne-Sioux expedition that annunciated an entire U.S. cavalry Column. In the same battle the magic of his war bonnet protected him when his horse got shoot underneath and he came out of the battle without a stitch on his body after he did not have his horse. They before the battle a Indian named White Bull warned him not to touch no metal to his lips and while he was reloading his six gun held bullet in his mouth because that his  brother died in the same battle and his friend died to his name was High Backed Wolf  if he did not touch the bullet to his mouth his friend and his  brother may not of died.

He had a lot of unflinching faith in the protective power of his war bonnet given to him by an old man named White Bull. So he had to follow a strict set of rules one of them was not to eat food that had contact with a metal, a white man's object a other was not to touch metal with his lips.

His most powerful medicine was his magnificent headdress he wore into battle it was made of one buffalo horn and one long trail of red and a black eagle feather it was believed to have absolute protective powers like could process a hypnotic power over his enemies and he immune to bullets. He believe that as long as he wore the bonnet and adhered to strict set of rules of conduct would protect him.
