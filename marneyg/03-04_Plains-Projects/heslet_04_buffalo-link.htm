The Buffalo
   Buffalo were very very important to the Apache.  It gave them almost everything they needed to live a healthy life.  Yes, they did farm as well, but buffalo was much more important.  Something's they couldn't make out of the buffalo, they traded with the Pueblos.  Another thing, is that since they were nomadic, they followed the buffalo a lot. They used the hide for wikinps, the bones for weapons and eating utensils, and the meat, they ate and traded it to other Indian tribes.
