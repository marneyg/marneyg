Germany began to use the Final Solution when Heydrich Himmler ordered that no person of Jewish decent could remain in one of the camps in Germany, they must be exterminated by a certain date.  Himmler also said If this can’t be done they are to be sent to ghettos in the other countries.  When Germany finally invaded and conquered the Soviet Union on June 22,1941. A new stage in the Holocaust began.

 

After the Germans conquered the Soviet Union they were confident of victory.  The Nazis developed a special killing force made up of SS units and police. They were to kill any Jews they could find in the occupied Soviet Union territory.  The special killing force took the Jewish town by surprise by acting swiftly.

 

When the Nazis conquer a town.  The order the victims to take off their clothes then march through terrain to were they were to be murdered by guns and then to be dumped into a single mass grave. 

 

Near Kiev at Babi Yar 34000 Jews were killed in two days.  The people who killed the most of the people killed because training and propaganda hardened them.  They were to vision the victims as enemies.  Some SS had to get drunk to do their actions, killing.

 

In the concentration camps they used Zyklon B to kill Jews.  The Germans only used it as punishment at first.  The Germans later used it just to killing Jews.  The Zyklon they used was produced at a firm called Degesch.

   

The Nazis established six extermination camps in Polish territory:  Chelmno, Belzec, Sobibor, Treblinka, Majdanek, and Auschwitz-Birkenau.  The Final Solution mainly consisted of: gassing, shooting, random acts of terror, disease, and starvation.  Those acts killed about six million Jews, about two-thirds of Germany’s Jewery.
