           During the sixties cars had many gadgets.  Chrome bumpers were a hot item for cars.  Most were equipped with push button gears, and a rambler.  Some had suicide knobs and doors while others had girly knobs.  At that time there was no power steering so the wheels turned two and a half times.  On most station wagons you were sure to find a "beaver panel", or a strip of wood along the car.
