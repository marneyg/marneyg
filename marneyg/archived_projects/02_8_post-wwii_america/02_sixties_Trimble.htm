The sixties were a groovy era when bell bottoms and the Beetles were in.  Back then McDonald's signs had the famous "more than 1,000,000 sold", and the malls were closed on Sunday.  The ice cream man wore white with the change on his belt, but subliminal advertising was legal.  Apollo 11 was one major event of the sixties.  It occurred on July 20, 1969.
