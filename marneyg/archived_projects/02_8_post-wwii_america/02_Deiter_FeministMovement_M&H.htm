Magazines and handbooks brought awareness to people who never paid must attention to the Feminist Movement before.  Between 1971 and 1976 "Our bodies Ourselves," a handbook by a women's health collective sold 850,000 copies.  Magazines also helped.  Gloria Steinem and several others founded a new magazine called, Ms., in 1972.
