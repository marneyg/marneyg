The 1970’s brought Disco with its bellbottoms and tight jeans.   Bikinis became smaller and tube socks became higher.   Polyester and iron-ons were popular.   Afros became bigger and colors became brighter.

 
Shoes ranged from chunky to soft and feminine.   Platforms were the biggest rave.   Seven to eight inch heels were common and rhinestones and sequins decorated all.
