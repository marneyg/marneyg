The Others Persecuted
"Kill without pity or mercy all men women and children of polish decent or language".  This is a quote from Adolf Hitler which may give you an idea of how gruesome about eleven million people where treated these people died.  Throughout the World this what has happened at that time is called the Holocaust. Many people automatically link it to the slaughter of the Jewish.  But that is only half true while yes five million Jews were put to death during the Holocaust a person who are "The Others" as they are commonly referred to.

Now before I go on you may want to know Hitler planned to make a ultimate race called the "Aryan" and that's why he had these people killed.  The Aryan race is based off the idea of Swiss men strong, blonde hair, blue eyes.  Although Hitler himself fit his idea of imperfect.

Well start off with the handicap.  When it comes to the handicap it was decided to be a waste of time and money to support them.  So in January of 1940 the entire Auschwitz killing enterprise began with the most helpless of all.  The institutionalized handicap were slain like cattle.  'This group included people who showed signs of mental illness, retardation, physical deformity, epilepsy, blindness, deafness, and severe alcoholism'  

Also like the Jewish Gypsies were chosen for total annihilation because of their race.  These people were called 'racially inferior and degenerate there for worthless."  One half of a million Gypsies "almost the entire European population" were wiped out during the holocaust.

Out of all the people homosexuals were problem treated the worst.  Many males from all countries were singled out and persecuted, raped, tortured, and executed.  

A good example of the hatred for these people, is that Hitler even sent his own men to concentration camps if he thought they were homosexual.  On concentration camps they were made to wear pink triangles so they could easily be found and be further humiliated. 

The people who were firmest to their beliefs are Jehovah Witness. From the very beginning Hitler was extremely threatened by them.  They refused to worship or believe in any other God besides Jehovah.  When they were asked to pledge loyalty to the Nazi ideology they refused and were intern imprisoned as “dangerous traitors” for not pledging loyalty to the Third Reich. 

Many Africans were brought into aid France during World War I.  After the war they had to take up residence in Europe.  A lot of them married white women.  Their children were called “Rhineland Bastard” and “Black Disgrace”.  These children were pulled out of school and if proven to be of African descent, they were forced to be sterilized without parents knowing.  They were also sent to concentration camps.  There were 300,000 – 400,000 people sterilized. 

Today almost half a century after the Holocaust, people still hardly know anything about “the others”.  But I hope the people that read this will tell others about these people whose lives have been taken will be remembered.
