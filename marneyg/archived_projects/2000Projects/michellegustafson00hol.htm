Adolf Hitler

      Adolf Hitler was born in Braunau, Austria April 20, 1889.  His mother and father died and he became an orphan.  In 1905 at the age of 16 he quit High school.  October, 1907 at the age of 19 he left home to go to Vienna.  On May 1913 at the age of 25 he had left Vienna to go to Munich.

      When war broke out in August 1914 when Hitler was 26, he joined Sixteenth Bavarian Infantry Regiment.  April 1920 he went to work full time at a party that was renamed National Socialist German Workers that you probable know  as a Nazi party.  There are still Nazi parties today.

      November 1923 - Several weeks before Joseph Goebbels was appointed the Minister of People's Enlightenment and Propaganda, Hitler was named chancellor of the new authoritarian regime.  Hitler was a highly skilled orator that spoke at the mass rallies.  Hitler thought people were most suggestible and least resistant.

      Hitler's philosophy about terror and was clear cut -

      ''We must be ruthless.... Only thus shall we purge (give) our people of their softness.... and their degenerate delight in beer - swilling.... I don't want the concentration camps transformed into penitentiaries.  Terror is the most effective political instrument .... It is my duty to make use of every means of training the German people to cruelty and to prepare them for war.... There must be no weakness or tenderness!''

      Hitler did NOT like Jews so he sat up concentration camps to kill all of the Jews.  Hitler said ''THERE MUST BE TOTAL REMOVAL OF THE JEWS!''

      Some people ask why do Germans listen to Hitler?  Well most Germans were scared of him and were afraid that Hitler would have the Nazis come and kill them!

      The Nazis respected Hitler, so they would salute him with their right arm held out at a strait angle.  They used it as a greeting witch meant ''hail'' witch is translated as ''saved'' or ''healed.'' When Hitler became leader of the Nazis the the greeting became ''Hail Hitler'' 

      Hitler had married to a young lady named Eva Braun who he had died with.  On April 29, 1945 3:25 p.m. Hitler gave his will to his secretary (Frau Sertrud Junge).  Hitler married Eva Braun 1hr. before they died together.

      This is all I got on Hitler's will it said-
''As I did not consider that I could take responsibility during the years of a struggle of my earthly career to take as my wife that girl who....''

     The next day on April 30, 1945 3:30 p.m. Adolf Hitler killed his wife Eva Braun then committed suicide.  Today people still fallow Hitler in Nazi parties.
