As you all know Adolf Hitler was a very cruel man.  He was the head of the Germans for several years.
     Hitler was born on April 20,1889 in Braunau am Inn.  His father died on January 3, 1903 and then his mother on December 21, 1907.  Hitler was homeless for four years after that (1909-1913).  Then in 1914 through 1918 he fought in World War 1.  The war ended with German defeat on November 11, 1918.  Hitler joined the German Workers Party in 1919.  The NAZI party was formed in 1920.  He was named the leader of the NAZI party in July, 1921.  On November 8, 1923 he tried to overthrow the government.
     On February 26, 1924 he was on trial for treason.  He was arrested that day and he started to write his book "Mein Kampf" or "My Struggle" while in his jail cell.  He was sentenced for 5 years but only served 9 months in which he dictated his book to Rudolf Hess.  The book was known as the NAZI bible.  By 1939 it had sold over 5 million copies and was translated into 11 languages.  He stated in his book "by defending myself against the work of the Jew, I am fighting for the work of the lord [p.60]."  This book was published in 1925.       
     In 1932 Hitler ran for president.  And on January 30, 1933 he was named chancellor of Germany, and on March 23, 1933 he became dictator of Germany.  He boycotted Jewish shops on April 1, 1933 and Dachau opened in the spring of 1933.  Hitler became Fuhrer on August 2, 1934.  He hosted the Olympics in Berlin, Germany in the summer of 1936.  He revealed his war plans on November 5, 1937.  In February 1938 he became army commander.  Hitler was named Man of the Year on September 29, 1938.
     Hitler thought pretty heavy of himself.  Here is some of the stuff he thought of himself: "I follow my course with the precision and security of a sleepwalker."  "I feel that no one in German history is as equipped as I am to bring the Germans to the position of supremacy which all German statesmen have felt the deserved but were unable to achieve."  "I carry out the commands that Providence has laid upon me.  But if the voice speaks then I know the time has come to act."  "Of secondary importance is the training of mental abilities.  Over-educated people, stuffed with knowledge and intellect, but bare of any sound instincts.  These impudent rascals who always know everything better than anybody else.  The intellect has grown autocratic and has become a disease of life."  "Do you realize you are in the presence of the greatest German of all time."  "I cannot be mistaken.  What I do and say is historical."  "For the last twenty-four hours I was the supreme court of the Germans."  "I am one of the hardest men Germany has had for decades, perhaps for centuries equipped with the greatest authority of any German Leader...but above all, I believe in my success. I believe in it unconditionally."  "I shall never make such a mistake I know how to keep my hold on people after I have passed on.  I shall be the Fuhrer they look up at and go home to talk of and remember.  My life shall not end in the mere form of death.  It will, on the contrary begin then."      
     This is what the Germans thought of Hitler: "When I had first met him his logic and sense of reality had impressed me, but as time went on he appeared to me to become more and more unreasonable and more and more convinced of his own infallibility ands greatness."  "His oratory used to wilt his collar, unglue his forelock, glaze his eyes; he was like a man hypnotized, repeating himself into a frenzy."  "We believe in Holy Germany!  Holy Germany is Hitler! We believe in Holy Hitler!"  "Hitler's word is god's law, the decrees and laws which represent it possesses divine authority."  "There has arisen a new authority as to what Christ and Christianity really are-that is Adolf Hitler...is the true Holy Ghost."
     Unfortunately Hitler survived an assassination attempt led by Claus von Stauffenberg on July 20, 1944.  About a year later he married his long time mistress Eva Braun, who tried to hang herself after a rift with Hitler in 1927.  They were married on April 29, 1945 and they both committed suicide the very next day.  Germans remembered him as the Psychopathic God.  To Hitler power was everything.
